Under Stone is a 2D platformer set in the depths of a medieval underground cave. The player ventures through dark rock passages, faces dangers lurking in the shadows, and uncovers the mysteries buried beneath the earth. With challenging level design and a dense, oppressive atmosphere, Under Stone tests the player's reflexes and determination with every jump and every step.

## Controls

<kbd>Left Arrow</kbd> - Move to the Left

<kbd>Right Arrow</kbd> - Move to the Right

<kbd>Z</kbd> - Dash

<kbd>X</kbd> - Jump

<kbd>C</kbd> - Attack
